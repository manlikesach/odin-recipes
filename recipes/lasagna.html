Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian dish layered with pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It's perfect for feeding a family or meal prepping for the week.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. Brown the beef.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 45 minutes.
  5. Let rest before serving.
